# 🌊 Harbor
Landing page and early validation for Harbor.

![Harbor Preview](./harbor.png)

A calm, safety-first dating experience built for disabled veterans.

---

## 🧭 Why Harbor exists

Most dating apps are designed for speed, engagement, and constant interaction.

For many people - especially disabled veterans - that can feel overwhelming, impersonal, or even unsafe.

Harbor is an attempt to create something different:
- slower
- more respectful
- focused on genuine connection over performance

---

## ⚓ What makes Harbor different

- **Safety-first design**  
  Built with clear boundaries, reporting tools, and user control in mind.

- **Intentional connections**  
  No endless swiping or pressure to perform.

- **Veteran-first approach**  
  Starting with a focused community to build trust and relevance.

## 📖 Project story

Harbor didn’t start as a business idea.

It started as a question:

**Why does something as human as connection feel so unnatural in modern dating apps?**

Many platforms reward speed, appearance, and constant interaction.
But not everyone moves through the world that way.

For disabled veterans - and others who value depth over performance -  
that environment can feel exhausting, isolating, or unsafe.

Harbor is an attempt to create a different kind of space.

Not louder.  
Not faster.  
Just more human.
